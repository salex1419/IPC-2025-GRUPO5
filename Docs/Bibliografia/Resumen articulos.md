1. **Food additive mixtures and type 2 diabetes incidence**: Mixtures of food additives are daily consumed worldwide by billions of people. So far, safety assessments have been performed substance by substance due to lack of data on the effect of multiexposure to combinations of additives. Our objective was to identify most common food additive mixtures, and investigate their associations with type 2 diabetes incidence in a large prospective cohort.
